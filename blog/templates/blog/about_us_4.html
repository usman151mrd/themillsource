How we make money

TMS (The Millennial Source Ltd.) is a privately held company with journalists, editors and marketers around the world. We make money through advertising. More specifically, our advertising revenue comes in the following forms:

Affiliate advertising partnerships:

These are advertising partnerships between us and companies non-for-profits. After meeting their management and marketing team, we test their product and profile their company and provide an honest account of both. If you make a purchase of their product through our platform, we earn a commission. Content that is sponsored by an affiliate partner is clearly labeled as such.
