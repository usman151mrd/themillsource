CASE STUDY

"Golf Athletics is a start-up that's only 9 months old. As we've recently developed our digital golf curriculum product (The Golf Athletics Blueprint) and proven its success with live examples, we were missing validation from the industry.

Having spoken to Krystal & her team from TMS, we briefed her team to help create a digital PR asset that we could use for the next 24 months. In our initial brand briefing, we explored our brand, our product and its complex values for golfers around the world. the TMS team was very patient, open and really immersed themselves in our brand, which is evident in the outcome of the digital PR project.

Krystal and her team utilized her network and found an amazing contact (an ex-tour professional). The article that was created surpassed our expectations. We managed to acquire an unbiased opinion about our brand and product that mirrored our values. For us this outcome is invaluable as it was genuine, insightful for our future customers and tells a story of our passion to help make golf more accessible to golfers around the world.

We have a mission to make golf easier to learn & play. Since having the PR asset, we have had many new opportunities for partnerships and an increased awareness & consideration of our brand.

The TMS team worked really hard to create a peice of PR that we will use to support our growth of the brand. We are extremely pleased!"

Kevin Hooi – Commercial Director, Co-Founder

The problem:

Golf Athletics had a clear understanding of their mission and had very quickly, proven their monetization strategy. However, they were struggling to validate their product within a legacy sport and also gain recognition beyond their market.

The campaign:

Understanding the situation, we spent two weeks coming up with different ways we could work with Golf Athletics to take their brand to the next level.

The campaign chosen by the Golf Athletics team involved reaching out to our network and connecting the product with an industry insider, an ex-golf pro.

We spent several weeks ensuring that our hand-picked source fully understood the USPs of the company’s product and Golf Athletics’ mission to maximize the chances of a positive, objective review.

It is with this that Golf Athletics were able to use the digital assets created from the campaign and open up more business opportunities.
